====================================
TROUBLESHOOTING
====================================

If you get "redirect_uri_mismatch":
- Double-check that the EXACT URI is in Google Cloud Console
- Make sure there are no trailing spaces or slashes
- Wait a full 3 minutes after saving in Google Console

If you get "access_denied":
- Make sure your email is added as a Test User in the OAuth consent screen
- Go to: https://console.cloud.google.com/apis/credentials/consent
- Click "Edit App" → go to "Test users" step → add your email

If you get "invalid_client":
- Check that GOOGLE_CLIENT_ID and GOOGLE_CLIENT_SECRET are set correctly
  in your Base44 environment variables
